From designing impactful marketing strategies and advancing financial inclusion in remote Indian villages to traveling across continents to master the art of data-driven decision-making.

I grew up in India, where I completed most of my education and built over five years of professional experience. I come from a family rooted in resilience and ambition — my father was the first in his family to earn a university degree, setting a powerful example that shaped my path. Raised with the belief that "you either win or you learn," I’ve carried this mindset through every personal and professional challenge. After earning a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication and an MBA in Finance, I took the unconventional route of preparing for competitive national banking exams — a demanding process that ultimately led to offers from three major public-sector banks. I began my career as a Deputy Manager at Bank of Maharashtra and later joined India Post Payments Bank (IPPB), where my journey in driving innovation and impact truly began.

The true turning point in my journey came when I joined India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) — a government-owned startup aiming to transform post offices into fully functional bank branches. As Assistant Manager – Strategy & Marketing, I was responsible for scaling operations across 190 branches, impacting millions of lives in underserved regions. Working across domains like strategic marketing, analytics, operations, and business development, I witnessed firsthand the deep gap in financial access between urban and rural India. That realization shaped my mission — to serve the unserved. Every day brought new challenges, pushing me to think creatively and lead with purpose. After 4.5 years of nation-building work, I chose to expand my impact by pursuing a Master’s in Marketing Analytics from the University of Maryland. There, I deepened my analytical skills and redefined my career to blend purpose with data-driven strategy. I remain committed to continuous growth and learning in my current role, consistently seeking opportunities to expand my skills and contribute more effectively.
